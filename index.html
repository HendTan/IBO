6GuyzCompany

6GuyzCompany has consistently established its place as a leading technological things branding in Malaysia since its establishment in 2019, and it does so now more than ever.

History

6GuyzCompany is a tech company launched on May 18, 2019. It was founded by Wong Sai Hei and his college roommates and fellow UCSI University students, in particular Eric Ik Zhen Chuan, Amit Sharma, Wong Soon Yoon, Ong Chun Siang and Chan Leong Hong. The membership was initially limited by the founders to UCSI students, but was expanded to other colleges in the Kuala Lumpur area, Melaka and gradually most universities in Malaysia, corporations and by September 2019, to everyone with a valid email address along with an age requirement of being 18 and older.

Mission

To provide the greatest personal computing products and services to students, educators, designers, scientists, engineers, business people, and consumers in more than 140 countries worldwide.

Vision

Our vision is to provide everyone in Malaysia with the latest and greatest tech where some of the other retailers don't.

Objective

Our goal is to hopefully become one of Malaysia's biggest electronics retailers by 2024.
